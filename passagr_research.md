### Ancestry/Heritage Citizenship Opportunities

**Still Available (2025-2026):**

- **Ireland:** Grandparent citizenship[^94]
- **Poland:** Parent, grandparent, or great-grandparent (if born/lived in Poland before/after Jan 1920 and didn't lose citizenship)[^95][^96][^97][^98]

**Recently Restricted:**

**Complex Requirements:**

- **Germany:** Generally parent only (post-1975 births)[^29]


### Protection/Asylum Pathways

**EU Asylum Procedures:** Under the new EU Migration and Asylum Pact (effective June 12, 2026), asylum applications within EU countries follow harmonized procedures:

- **Border Procedure:** Screening and decision within 12 weeks for applicants from safe countries or with low recognition likelihood[^21][^22][^99]
- **Regular Procedure:** 6 months for full examination[^22][^21]
- **Key First Step:** Physical presence required; register at designated immigration office or border facility[^9][^10]

**Non-EU Countries:**

- **Canada:** Refugee Status Determination via IRCC/IRB; claim at port of entry or inland[^61][^9]
- **Australia, New Zealand:** Protection visa application; must be in country or offshore UNHCR referral[^9]
- **Uruguay, Argentina, Mexico:** National refugee commissions (CONARE/COMAR) handle RSD with UNHCR support[^9]
